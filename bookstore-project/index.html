Our Story

Elma Printers and Stationers (EPS) started out in 2017. It started as a cyber then to a bookstore by extension, now a full-fledged bookshop. It was founded by Josephine Mwololo as a sole entity. Elma Printers is growing to be a trusted bookstore meeting the demands of its client and growing its capacity to meet the demands of Kenya's vibrant education sector.

Elma Printers has one outlet in Kimilili dealing with wholesale and retail.

Moving Forward

Elma Printers encourages the introduction of new products inline with current market trends by embracing technology.
